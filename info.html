Information

Who I am

A short introduction

My name is Thomas. I'm 18 years old and I study Software Development at ROC Midden Nederland.

Where I'm based

I'm from the Netherlands — born in Utrecht and raised there until around age seven or eight, when my mother and I moved to a small town in the countryside where we still live.

Why software development?

I enjoy working with computers. I spend a lot of my free time on my PC — playing games and experimenting with mods — and that led me to programming. After high school I briefly started learning to become a car mechanic, but after about 1.5 years I decided it wasn't for me and switched to software development. Since then I've been learning web technologies like HTML, CSS, PHP and SQL, and I have been enjoying the learning process.



My journey into tech

How it began

I've always been interested in electronics and computers. My first gaming experiences were on a Wii, later I used an older Windows 7 laptop to experiment with modding. At 16 I bought a second-hand PC and upgraded it over time, that experience deepened my interest in modding and coding.

What keeps me motivated

Seeing my progress as i work keeps me motivated: when I build a website and refresh to see changes, that immediate feedback is satisfying. I also enjoy problem solving, debugging and finding solutions is rewarding, and the idea of doing work I enjoy motivates me.



Skills and interests

Technologies I enjoy

I find web development with HTML, CSS and PHP especially interesting. Turning a plain white and black page into something interactive and visually appealing feels powerful. CSS in particular lets you give a site character and life.

Preferences

I personally prefer frontend work, I enjoy building the visual side with HTML and CSS more than backend tasks, although I find backend topics like PHP and SQL interesting as well. In the future I'd like to explore game development and desktop apps.

Hobbies

Outside of coding I'm fascinated by military technology and aviation, especially military aircraft. I also enjoy playing video games, some of my favourites include Forza Horizon, GTA V and Red Dead Redemption 2.



Goals

Short term

In the short term I want to finish my education and obtain my diploma. After that I'd like to work in web development and continue improving my skills, including learning JavaScript and C.

Longer term / dream projects

I have a few dream projects: one is a platform for developers to share projects and get feedback (something this site could evolve into). I'd also like to build a game or a mobile app one day.
